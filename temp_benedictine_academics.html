Academics at Benedictine University include 42 undergraduate majors, 9 graduate degrees, 2 doctoral programs, and 17 certificate programs. Ben-U has something for everyone.

At Ben-U, we aspire to nurture independent thinkers who go on to lead lives of meaning and purpose.

Whether you’ve set your career goals in motion, or you’re still pondering your future, Benedictine has a path forward for you.

The BenU Vision For Your Education

Affordable

67% of our students receive student aid, making Ben-U one of the most affordable small private universities in the Midwest.

Community-Focused

Our small campuses and vibrant student communities are the perfect environments for making friends, getting to know professors, and building professional networks.

Career Ready

Benedictine’s academic programs prioritize work-integrated learning so our students can graduate with real-world experience in their chosen fields.

Education Programs

Study Abroad

Become a citizen of the world with a Ben-U study abroad program.

Explore Opportunities

Faculty Led Programs

Faculty-led programs provide an opportunity for the classroom to go global. Courses are embedded into the experience abroad for a hands-on learning opportunity.

Fee-Based Programs

Choose from a variety of programs in various international locations to immerse yourself in. From Italy to England to Beijing, the options present you with new cultural experiences.

Exchange Programs

BenU has developed exchange programs with institutions around the world. These programs are unique because they allow students to participate in a semester, yearlong or summer program abroad while paying their regular tuition.

Third-Party Provider Programs

Benedictine University works with a number of organizations that offer additional study abroad opportunities for interested students.
